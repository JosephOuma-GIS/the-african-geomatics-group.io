About The African Geomatics Group

Precision. Insight. Impact.

The African Geomatics Group is a premier geospatial consulting firm headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, specializing in advanced spatial analysis, remote sensing, and GIS solutions tailored for African development challenges.

Led by Joseph Otieno Ouma, a renowned geospatial expert with over a decade of experience across East Africa, China, and West Africa, we bridge cutting-edge technology with local knowledge to deliver actionable insights.

Our Services

End-to-end geospatial solutions from data acquisition to decision-ready insights
